About Us

A brief description of our club

GMMSC ICT Club

The GMMSC ICT Club is a vibrant group of students who are passionate about technology and innovation.
We focus on creating a space where members can learn, grow, and collaborate through hands-on projects and events. Our goal is to develop skills, foster creativity, and build leadership, while tackling real-world challenges together.

Whether you're into coding, problem-solving, or simply exploring new tech, the club is here to support your journey and prepare you for the future.

Our Departments

We facilitate the members of our club in the following Departments

Programming

Programming in Python, C, Java etc, Cyber Security, Web Development, SysAdmin, Networking, Script writing fall under Programming Department

Content Creation

Graphic Design, Video Editing, Social Media Management, Presentation Creation, Storytelling, Digital Art, Animations etc are included in the Content Creation Department

IT

MS Office Tools, Hardware Troubleshooting, System Maintenance, Database Handling, Hardware Infrastructure are the concerns of IT Department

Gaming

Gaming Department helds occassional gaming tournaments and challanges. Gameplay submitted by members are showcased in our platforms
